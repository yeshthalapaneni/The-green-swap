Step 1

Consumerism

Consumerism means buying more products than we really need.

Why it happens

Advertising, social pressure, and fast trends encourage frequent buying.

What it causes

More production, more resource use, and more waste.

Better choice

Buy only what you need, choose durable products, and repair when possible.
