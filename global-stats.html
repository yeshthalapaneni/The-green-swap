Global Stats: Consumerism and Waste

Recent global figures from major institutions show the scale of waste linked to consumption patterns.

2.01 billion tonnes

Municipal solid waste generated globally each year (World Bank estimate, baseline period around 2016).

3.40 billion tonnes by 2050

Projected annual municipal solid waste if current trends continue (World Bank).

62 million tonnes

E-waste generated in 2022; only 22.3% documented as formally collected and recycled (ITU/UNITAR Global E-waste Monitor 2024).

353 million tonnes

Plastic waste generated globally in 2019 (OECD Global Plastics Outlook).

9% recycled

Share of global plastic waste recycled in 2019 (OECD Global Plastics Outlook).

Sources: World Bank What a Waste; ITU/UNITAR Global E-waste Monitor 2024; OECD Global Plastics Outlook.
