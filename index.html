Our mission

The Green Swap is built to make sustainable living simple and practical. By enabling item-to-item exchange, we reduce unnecessary purchases, prevent usable products from being discarded, and strengthen local circular economies.

How it works

1. List what you have

Add details and condition so others can quickly understand your item.

2. Find what you need

Browse available swaps and connect with people who match your needs.

3. Swap responsibly

Exchange directly, reduce waste, and keep valuable products in use.

Built for long-term impact

Every successful swap means one less item in landfill and one less new product that needs to be manufactured. The Green Swap promotes a low-waste lifestyle through small, consistent, human decisions.
