Waste Products

Waste products are materials thrown away after use, including plastic packaging, e-waste, textiles, and food waste.

Main categories

  • Plastic waste: bags, bottles, wrappers, and single-use containers.
  • E-waste: discarded electronics like phones, laptops, chargers, and batteries.
  • Textile waste: clothing discarded due to fast fashion cycles.

Why it matters

Poor waste management leads to landfill overflow, pollution, and health risks. Reducing waste is key to a cleaner environment.
