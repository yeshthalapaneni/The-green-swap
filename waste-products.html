Waste Products

Waste products are items we throw away after use, such as plastic, e-waste, and old clothing.

Plastic waste

Single-use packaging that remains in nature for a long time.

E-waste

Discarded electronics that need safe disposal.

Textile waste

Clothes discarded quickly due to fast fashion.
