Waste Products

Waste products are materials discarded after use, such as plastics, textiles, food leftovers, and electronic waste.

If waste is not reduced or managed correctly, it causes pollution in land, water, and air.

Reducing waste starts with reusable products, responsible sorting, and avoiding unnecessary packaging.

Major types

Plastic

Long-lasting synthetic materials that accumulate rapidly.

E-waste

Discarded devices that may contain hazardous elements.

Textile

Clothing waste from fast-fashion consumption cycles.
