The Green Swap

The Green Swap is a practical solution: exchange useful items instead of always buying new products.

How it helps

  • Extends product life and reduces unnecessary disposal.
  • Reduces demand for new manufacturing and packaging.
  • Saves money for individuals and supports community sharing.

Examples

Books, furniture, household tools, clothes, and electronics in usable condition can all be swapped responsibly.
